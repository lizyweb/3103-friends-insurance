Types of Motor Insurance

Third-Party Insurance

Compulsory insurance that provides compensation for damages to other vehicles and bodily injuries.

Comprehensive Insurance

Covers all damages to the vehicle and third-party damages.

Own Damage Insurance

Covers only damages to your own vehicle.

Depreciation Waiver Insurance

Insurance that covers the full value of old parts without depreciation.

Pay-Per-Mile Insurance

Insurance premium calculated based on the distance traveled.

Basic Third-Party Liability Insurance

Basic insurance for third-party damages.
